**SmartForms vs CoPilots: When to Use Which**

If you're scratching your head about the difference, here's the simple version. SmartForms are transactional. User fills out form, AI generates output, done. They're fast, focused, and perfect when you know exactly what inputs you need to produce a specific result.

CoPilots are conversational. The AI acts like a consultant who's been trained on your methodology. Users can ask questions, get clarification, explore different angles. It's the difference between using a calculator versus having a tutor sit next to you.

Most successful FormWise users mix both in their toolsets. Maybe you have a SmartForm that generates initial draft content, and a CoPilot that helps users refine their brand voice. Or a SmartForm for quick social media captions, and a CoPilot for broader marketing strategy conversations.

The platform supports something called prompt chaining, which is basically linking multiple AI steps together. So your SmartForm might gather user input, generate a first draft, analyze that draft for improvements, then spit out the polished version. All happening automatically in the background.
